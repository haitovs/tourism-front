International Forum 2026

We are pleased to welcome you to the official page of the International Tourism Exhibition and Forum “Tourism and Travel – 2026.”

This premier event is strategically scheduled for Spring 2026 in the majestic capital of Turkmenistan, the city of Ashgabat. The Forum and Exhibition are positioned as a crucial international platform dedicated to showcasing and advancing the dynamic development of the tourism sector.

The primary mission of the event is to vividly present the rich and diverse tourism potential of Turkmenistan to the global community, while simultaneously facilitating a robust and comprehensive exchange of best international practices and experience in travel and hospitality.

The agenda is specifically focused on key growth areas, including:

  • Strengthening bilateral and multilateral cooperation with foreign partners across the entire tourism ecosystem.
  • Addressing challenges and opportunities within the hospitality and hotel business sector.
  • Promoting the integration of modern technologies through the digitalization of tourist services.
  • Fostering deeper cultural and humanitarian ties through travel.

Participation offers industry professionals a unique and essential opportunity for high-level professional dialogue, establishing valuable new business contacts, securing investments, and defining the strategic outlook for the tourism industry's sustainable development in the region.
